81539 Impossibilities to Possibilities

Preparation

What is impossible?

Summary

Bring to the first class an A4 page with very short story about something that originally seemed impossible but has been overcome.

Deadline:9:00am Monday 4 July 2016

Deliverable

On the first day of class, bring a short story about a problem or idea that was originally thought to be impossible but that later became possible.

We would love to hear a personal story from your own life. However, you can share any story that inspires you.

  • Prepare your story on a single page of A4 paper.
  • Keep it short and compelling.
  • If you’re writing: use large fonts or clear handwriting and no more than a paragraph or two of text.
  • Feel free to tell your story with cartoons or stick-figures.

Marking Criteria

This is not assessed. However, it is an essential requirement for the activities planned for Day 1.
